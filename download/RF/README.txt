RF is a tiny parallel corpus of the Declarations of the Swedish
Government and its translations. The corpus was originally compiled by
Erik Tjong Kim Sang (At Uppsala University at that time) and mainly
serves as a small example corpus and for educational purposes. More
information about the corpus can be found at http://opus.nlpl.eu/RF.php

xx.tar.gz ........... tokenized corpus files in language <xx> (XML format)
xx.raw.tar.gz ....... untokenized (raw) corpus files
xx.parsed.tar.gz .... UD-parsed corpus files
xx-yy.xml.gz ........ standoff sentence alignments files in XCES format
xx-yy.txt.zip ....... aligned plain text files (Moses format)
xx-yy.tmx.gz ........ bilingual TMX files

sub-directories:

mono ................ monolingual plain text files
freq ................ word frequencies
xx-yy ............... word alignments and phrase-tables
ud .................. word alignments of UD-parsed corpora
